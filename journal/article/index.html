Amano Hotel London — A Case Study in Atmospheric Hospitality Lighting

AUTHOR + DATE · Jost Light 10px · #78726C

By Sarah Whitmore  ·  12 June 2026

When Illuminati's studio team was briefed on the Amano Hotel project in late 2024, the ask was deceptively simple: make the light feel like it belongs. What followed was fourteen months of collaboration between our studio, the interior design team at Heathfield & Co, and the project architects, Stiff + Trevillion.

H2 · Outfit Regular 24px · --color-primary

The Brief: Atmosphere Over Specification

Unlike most commercial hospitality projects, the Amano brief led with atmosphere rather than lux levels. The design director had one instruction: every space should feel like the light was always there. That constraint shaped every decision we made — from fixture selection to circuit design to colour rendering index.

We specified CRI 95+ sources throughout, even in back-of-house areas visible to guests. The added cost was marginal against total M&E spend. The perceptual difference was not.

PULL QUOTE · Outfit Regular 20px · brass · 2px brass left border · pl 24px · my 40px
The most important lighting decision in any room is the one that goes unnoticed.
H3 · Outfit Regular 20px · --color-primary

Across 14 Floors, 312 Rooms

The scale of the project required a modular approach. We developed a core palette of six fixture types — three pendant variants, two wall brackets, and an integrated cove profile — that could be combined across guest rooms, corridors, public areas, and the rooftop bar.

IMAGE CAPTION · Jost Light 10px · --color-muted · text-align centre

The main corridor, Level 7. ILL-SIG-P3 pendant, Aged Brass. Photo: Amano Studios.

The rooftop presented the greatest challenge. South-facing, exposed, with a nine-metre glazed facade, it needed to read as warm and intimate after dark while meeting planning authority requirements for light spill. Our solution: a recessed perimeter cove combined with individually dimmed pendants on a two-circuit system.

  • —ILL-SIG-P3 pendant, Aged Brass, 3000K, CRI 97
  • —ILL-ARC-W2 wall bracket, custom matte bronze powder coat
  • —ILL-COV-9 integrated LED cove profile, tunable white 2700–4000K
  • —Casambi wireless control throughout, zone-addressable per room

The project completed in February 2026. Guest reviews have mentioned the atmosphere of the rooms more frequently than any other design element — which, for a lighting company, is perhaps the best review of all.
